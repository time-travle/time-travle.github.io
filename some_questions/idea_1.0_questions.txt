3、html 格式化时 boday head 无法缩进
    https://blog.csdn.net/qq_42143163/article/details/99312857
    1.找到File>Settings
    2.找到Settings>Editor>HTML
    3.找到HTML>Other>Do not indent children on
    4.把html、body、head删除掉即可，点击ok即可
